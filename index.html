How the calculation works:
• Salary proration: Fixed salary is divided by total calendar days × payable days (calendar days − unpaid leave − rest day deduction).
• Rest day deduction: 0.4 days per unpaid leave day taken (e.g. 2 unpaid days → 0.8 rest day deduction).
• Attendance bonus: Retained if unpaid leave ≤ 2 days.
• Performance commission: Based on actual working days entered × cases per day × rate. Not affected by rest day deductions.
